Using AI Responsibly

AI is a powerful tool. Using it ethically and with awareness is key to a healthy relationship with this technology.

Your Data, Your Control

You have control over your data. You can opt out of having your chats used for model training at any time in `Settings > Data Controls`. For private conversations, use "Temporary Chat," which doesn't save to your history or use memory.

A Guide, Not a Replacement

Remember, ChatGPT is a supportive tool, not a therapist or a substitute for human connection. For high-stakes personal decisions, use it to help you think through options, not to get a direct answer. It's here to assist your judgment, not replace it.
